Superior

The room is 27 square meters and consists of one living room with carpet floor and a bathroom with shower (all rooms with large balcony and pool view). The room has one double bed or two single beds, a desk with an armchair and a classic wardrobe. For the convenience of the guest there is satellite TV + smart TV, central air conditioning, heating, safe, mini bar, telephone, towels, slippers, bathrobes, hair dryer and cosmetic set. It is possible to deliver an extra bed. Room rates include buffet breakfast, one parking space on site and Wi-Fi.
